COVID help sites for India to fight the massive wave it is going through.

Sites which look for available hospitals, doctor numbers, food delievery options, oxygen, drug supplier info etc around you:

https://covid19-twitter.in - Finds hospitals, Beds, oxygen etc available at hospitals through twitter searches.
https://covidfacts.in        - Does the same as above but through crowdsourced data.

https://covidfightclub.org - Finds hospitals and people who can fullfil your COVID-19 related health requirements


https://covid19helpindia.in - Finds avaliable free ambulances with their phone numbers, helplines, drug supplier contact info etc. You can search by Resource type and state to filter.

https://covid-resources-chi.vercel.app - Finds hospitals and doctor's contacts which fullfils the specific requirement you are looking for (available beds, oxygen etc)


https://hydcovidresources.com/ - Finds hospitals, Beds, oxygen etc available at hospitals through twitter searches.

https://covidamd.com/ - Finds hospitals with beds and plasma avaibility in Ahmedabad


https://covidtools.in/    - Finds oxygen, hospitals with available beds, plasma, tocilizumab/COVID related drug supplier info, ambulance numbers, consultation, tiffin/food delivery in 16 states.
